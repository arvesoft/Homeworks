Question ask that $P\{ S | N \}$\\

By using Bayes and Law of Total Probability rules we get that:\\

 $P\{ S | N \} = P\{ N | S \} * P\{ S \} / (P\{ N | S \} * P\{ S \} + P\{ N | \overline{S} \} * P\{ \overline{S} \})$ \\
 
= $0.1 * 0.05 / (0.1 * 0.05 + 0.98 * 0.95)$\\

= $0.005341$
